About me

I am a restless and curious developer who enjoys learning and understanding how things work. For years I have worked in the civil engineering industry where I learned how to prioritize my tasks while developing constantly changing projects. As a professional, I have realized that productivity is strictly related to how well your team members communicate with each other. Therefore, I truly believe that to succeed in the IT industry we must be professionals with not only coding skills but also management abilities and empathy for each other.

As a Software Developer, I’m eager to give back to my community which has always supported me and many others. Hence, I plan to use my skills to improve society, businesses and people’s lives. That being said, I'm currently looking for an opportunity to join an inclusive and collaborative team.

In addition, I’m a huge fan of animes, video games, camping, outdoor activities and travelling which I certainly spend too many hours planning, in detail, even though I might not even know how ($) or when I would go on that adventure.

Thanks for getting to know me a little bit more hopefully we’ll soon be sharing some laughs about how life is unpredictable or any other random subject.

Projects

  • Dragonfly

    REACT | NODE.JS | POSTGRES | HEROKU | NETLIFLY

    A Co.lab project created by a cross-function team; this website helps the users to manage their job applications. Users can create their profile and add several card info for each job application that they have applied or intends to apply. They can manually enter all info or copy and paste a URL link from LinkedIn that will scrap the info and add it to the card. An MVP has been released but as a team we continue working to improve and add further features to the website.
